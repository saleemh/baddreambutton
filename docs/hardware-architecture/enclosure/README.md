# Enclosure Notes

The Bad Dream Button enclosure should optimize for bedside usability before miniaturization.

## Early Mechanical Goals

- easy to find and press in the dark
- simple to attach to a bed frame, nightstand, or wall
- battery access without damaging the shell
- room for a larger external button cap than the PCB switch itself

## Early Mounting Ideas

- adhesive-backed bedside puck
- keyhole or screw mount for fixed placement
- clip-on shell for bed frame attachment

## 3D Printing Direction

A practical first enclosure is:

- two-piece shell
- top cap with generous press area
- internal standoffs for a small prototype PCB or breakout
- removable door or slide access for battery replacement

## Design Constraint

If the product stays on `CR2032`, enclosure space will be heavily influenced by:

- coin-cell holder size
- antenna clearance
- the physical size needed for a reassuring bedside press surface

That tradeoff should be explored alongside the electrical prototype rather than after it.
